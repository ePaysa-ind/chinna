Privacy Policy - Chinna: AI Crop Assistant

Last Updated: May 20, 2025

Welcome to Chinna, an AI-powered crop assistant designed to help Indian farmers identify pests and diseases in their crops. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Summary: Chinna collects minimal personal information (phone number, name, village name), uses your camera only for crop analysis, and stores most data locally on your device. We use Google's Gemini AI for crop analysis but do not share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Used for authentication purposes only
  • Name: To personalize your experience
  • Village: To provide location-relevant farming advice
  • Crop Information: The type of crops you're growing and when they were sown
  • Acreage: To tailor advice to your farming scale

1.2 Images and Media

  • Crop Images: Photos you take or upload for pest/disease identification
  • Camera Access: Used only when you actively choose to take photos for analysis
  • Gallery Access: Used only when you choose to upload existing images

1.3 Usage Information

  • App Interactions: How you use the app's features
  • Analysis History: Records of previous pest/disease identifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered pest and disease identification services
  • To offer personalized crop management practices
  • To improve the app's functionality and accuracy
  • To authenticate your account using phone verification
  • To store your preferences and settings
  • To provide location-based weather data for your area
  • To customize agricultural advice based on your soil type and local conditions

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Chinna prioritizes offline-first functionality. Most of your data, including:

  • User information (name, village, crop details)
  • Analysis history (recent scans with confidence scores)
  • Crop practice schedules

is stored locally on your device, not on our servers.

3.2 Firebase Services

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication: To verify your phone number (Firebase Auth)

3.3 Google Services

We use Google services for the following purposes:

  • Gemini AI: To analyze crop images. When you submit an image for analysis:
    • Your image is securely transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service
    • The analysis results are returned to your device
    • No personally identifiable information is attached to images sent for analysis
  • Weather API: To provide local weather data:
    • Your device location or village information may be used to fetch weather data
    • The app requests current weather conditions from Google Weather API
    • This information helps provide context-specific agricultural advice
    • Weather data is cached locally to reduce API calls

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted services that help us operate (Firebase, Gemini AI)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of future communications
  • Update your information through the app settings

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To capture crop images for analysis
  • Storage: To access your gallery for image selection
  • Internet: For authentication, AI analysis, and weather services
  • Location: To provide location-specific weather data and agricultural advice

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Most data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
